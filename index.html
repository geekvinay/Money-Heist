DIRECTOR MONEY HEIST

Álex Pina (Pamplona) is a television producer, writer, series creator, scriptwriter and Spanish film director, known recently for his global hit crime drama Money Heist. Previous shows include Vis a Vis (Locked Up), El embarcadero (The Pier), and Los hombres de Paco (Paco's Men). He began his career as a journalist in the press ( Diario Vasco , Diario de Mallorca ...) and later in the Europa Press Agency .

Creations : Money Heist, Sky Rojo, White Lines.
